# QuantumEdge Alpha

> Institutional-grade quant tools and intelligent automation for the average Joe. Because your 401k shouldn't fund a hedge fund's yacht.

## Overview

QuantumEdge Alpha is an open-source project dedicated to democratizing quantitative trading tools and market intelligence. We're building the infrastructure that levels the playing field between retail investors and institutional market makers.

**Website:** [https://okcwade1-lab.github.io/](https://okcwade1-lab.github.io/)

---

## 🎯 Mission

Retail investors are the liquidity. The wealth gap isn't accidental — it's a feature. We're fixing the information asymmetry, one tool at a time.

### Why This Exists

- **Institutional models are hidden** - Market makers use Greeks (GEX, DEX, Vanna, Charm) to navigate markets. Retail doesn't have access to this.
- **Regime detection is powerful** - Most traders chase noise. We identify market structure using HMM, Markov chains, and Bayesian inference.
- **Position sizing matters** - Having an edge means nothing if you blow up. Kelly Criterion sizing keeps you in the game.
- **Automation saves time** - AI-powered workflows scan markets so you don't have to stare at screens 24/7.

---

## 📦 Core Features

### 📊 **Greeks & Gamma Exposure**
Institutional-grade exposure models for understanding where the pins are set and who's hedging what.

### 🧠 **Regime Detection**
HMM, Markov chains, and Bayesian inference to identify market regimes before they're obvious on the chart. Act on structure, not noise.

### ⚡ **Intelligent Automation**
AI-powered workflows that scan, analyze, and surface opportunities. Spend less time staring at screens and more time making decisions.

### 🎯 **Kelly-Sized Edge**
Fractional Kelly position sizing baked in. Risk-adjusted position management to optimize growth while managing drawdown.
